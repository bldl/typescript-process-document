The TypeScript Suggestion/Proposal Stages
TC39 Equiv. Stage Purpose Entrance Criteria Expected Outcomes Spec Quality at Entrance Post-Stage Changes Expected Implementation Expected Expected Feedback from Users
0 Untriaged Allow community members to come up with new feature suggestions. Open discussions and consider authoring a more detailed proposal. None TypeScript Design Team will discuss this suggestion, or request a proposal. N/A Major None Considerable.

Discussions at this stage happen in public Github issue threads.

1, 2 In Discussion TypeScript Design Team discusses the suggestion. What are the entrance criteria?

It seems that a proposal does not necessarily have to exist at this stage.

TypeScript Design Team has internal discussions.

TypeScript Design Team has accepted the proposal/feature, and implementation may begin.

(Early) Draft Major None None.

However, the GitHub issue for the suggestion remains open, allowing for further comments.

On Hold Awaiting More Feedback Wait for feedback from community members who would benefit from the suggestion. TypeScript Design Team must gather more feedback from community members in order to make a decision. The community has voiced its opinion on the suggestion, and TypeScript Design Team may proceed with discussions. What is the spec quality at entrance? Major None Considerable.

This stage exists for community members to voice their opinions and conduct open discussions.

Needs Investigation Allow a team member to investigate the suggestion. What are the entrance criteria? A direction for the design of the suggestion will be worked out. What is the spec quality at entrance? Major None.

However, the GitHub issue for the suggestion remains open, allowing further comments.

Needs Proposal Request that a detailed and formal proposal be authored for the suggestion. The proposal will be used to more accurately evaluate the suggestion. What are the entrance criteria? A proposal has been authored by the original author or another person. Draft.

The template is filled out, but is too imprecise / incomplete / informal for TypeScript Design Team to properly evaluate.

Major None.

However, the GitHub issue for the suggestion remains open, allowing further comments.

Needs More Info Address questions in the proposal. Questions have been identified regarding the suggestion, and they have been relayed to the author. The questions have been addressed, and discussions may continue. Draft.

A formal proposal exists, but has unresolved questions.

Major None.

However, the GitHub issue for the suggestion remains open, allowing further comments.

Waiting for TC39 Signal that the suggestion is not actionable before TC39 has reached some conclusion on a related proposal. Revisit Signal that a suggestion that has been put on hold earlier, is now worth reconsidering. What are the entrance criteria? The suggestion has been taken back into discussion. What is the spec quality at entrance? (Very) Major What feedback is expected from users?
